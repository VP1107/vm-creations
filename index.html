Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide hosting and domain? +
We can help you set up hosting and a domain. While the costs are separate, we guide you through the best affordable options (like GitHub Pages or Netlify for hosting).
How many revisions are included? +
We offer 2-3 rounds of revisions to ensure you are 100% happy with the final result.
Do you maintain the website after launch? +
Since we build static sites, maintenance is minimal. However, we offer support for small updates for 30 days post-launch for free.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept UPI, Bank Transfer, and other standard digital payment methods. Payment: 50% upfront, 50% on completion.
What if I need changes after 30 days? +
We offer affordable maintenance packages for updates beyond the free 30-day period. Contact us for pricing.
Do you work with WordPress? +
We specialize in static websites (HTML, CSS, JS) which are faster and more secure. WordPress projects can be discussed on a case-by-case basis.
What about SSL certificates? +
All our recommended hosting options (GitHub Pages, Netlify) include free SSL certificates for secure HTTPS connections.
